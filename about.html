Company Introduction

Founded in Hong Kong in 2020, MYADSMOBI has rapidly grown into a team of over 150 professionals with offices in seven cities around the world. Our mission is to connect China with global markets, helping clients create innovative business models and accelerate growth.

At MYADSMOBI, we proudly offer cutting-edge mobile-first programmatic solutions for both branding and performance marketing needs. Our platform stands out with complete transparency, strong technical foundations, and comprehensive programmatic management tools. Leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, MYADSMOBI focuses on delivering tangible results, addressing the complex challenges faced by marketers today.
